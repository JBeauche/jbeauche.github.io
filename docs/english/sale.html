For Sale

The horses available for sale come from our breeding program. Our breeding program aims to breed and train top-quality Warmbloods. Vintage Oasis Farm is committed to producing athletic horses with great working minds and conformation. We only choose stallions that complement the conformation and abilities of our mares to obtain versatile sport horses in all disciplines of English riding.

Our horse starting and training program focuses on listening to the horse and on their physical development by paying special attention to equine biomechanics. Our aim is that each of our horses obtains flexible, powerful, and balanced movements; giving our horses all the necessary tools so that they can perform in their discipline.

Each of our horses is up to date on vaccination, farrier, and dewormer.

Contact us for pricing and for any other inquiries.
