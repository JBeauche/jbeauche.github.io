Horse Boarding

Vintage Oasis Farm's indoor and outdoor board services include unlimited access to hay and water (heated in the winter), salt lick, saddle and bridle rack as well as extra storage space. Access to an emergency box stall is also available for all boarders.

Pricing (monthly, excluding taxes)
  • Outdoor board: $550.00
  • Indoor board: $650.00
Services at extra costs
  • Blanketing services (outdoor board only)
  • Standing for farrier and veterinarian
  • Feeding grain (outdoor board only). Owners must supply their own grain and supplements
