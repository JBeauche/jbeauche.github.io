Horse Starting Training Rehabilitation

We are offering the following training services: starting young horses, training/schooling, rehabilitation, including readying for shows for jumper, hunter, dressage, and eventing horses.

Our horse starting and training program focuses on listening to the horse and on their physical development by paying special attention to equine biomechanics. Our aim is that each of our horses obtains flexible, powerful, and balanced movements; giving our horses all the necessary tools so that they can perform in their discipline.

Turnkey services

  • Desensitization
  • Gentle, positive, and effective methods; including Tellington TTouch method
  • Indoor board
