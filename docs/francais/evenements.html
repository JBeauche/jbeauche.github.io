avec le Centre de Services Équilibre

Initiation a la zoothérapie?

Vous aimeriez en savoir plus sur la zoothérapie ou étudier dans ce domaine?

Alors notre formation “ Initiation à la zoothérapie “ est faite pour vous !

L'atelier se déroule sur 2 journées complètes de perfectionnement qui vous aideront à développer ces habiletés et vivre ces deux journées en tant que zootherapeutes d'un jour.

Cette formation vous donne une attestation de réussite et le préalable pour vous inscrire à notre programme de formation.

Ces deux jours ont lieu ici, à la Ferme Vintage Oasis à St- Jean sur Richelieu de 9h à 16h.

Le tarif régulier est de 299$ plus taxes mais un rabais de 50$ est applicable à l'inscription.

Pour en savoir plus ou vous inscrire, contactez le Centre de Services Équilibre

19 et 20 août - Clinique de Dressage

avec Lynda Trenholme

Lynda is a highly experienced dressage coach, trainer, and judge, with a passion for helping riders achieve their goals. At our clinic, she'll provide personalized instruction to help you improve your technique, balance, and connection with your horse.

Individual lessons with Lynda are available for dressage riders of all levels, from low level to Grand Prix. And if you're not riding, you're welcome to come and audit the lessons and learn from the sidelines.

About Lynda

Lynda Trenholme has been an FEI dressage competitor and trainer for over 30 years. “I began my career over 40 years ago, rode Western, bought an Arabian, learned to jump and then discovered dressage.”

Lynda has won numerous awards, such as the Fédération équestre du Québec Athlete of the Year in Dressage in 1991, and championships at all levels in dressage from basics to Grand Prix. She has also trained 10 horses, starting them as 4-year-olds right up to the international level, including the Grand Prix level.

“I have taken clinics over my riding career with many Grand masters, Olympians such as Stephen Clarke (England), Bimbo Peillicle (Germany), Johan Hinneman (Germany), Michalais (Germany), Robert Dover’s assistant (USA), and Tom Poulin (USA).”

Lynda has worked with numerous top trainers, (to name a few) Jacqueline Brookes, Gina Smith, Ashley Holzer, Cindy Ishoy, Tom Dvorak, Simmons Williams and Christilot Boylen, who was my coach for many years.

Lynda has given clinics all over Quebec, Ontario, Maritimes, and the United States.

Lynda has taken Natural Horsemanship courses with Richard Thomson, hunted with the Lake of Two Mountains Hunt, and given Grand Prix Dressage demonstrations before the Grand Prix jumping at Blainville.

Lynda was also a medium-level dressage judge by Equestrian Canada for over 30 years and is now a licensed coach.
