English Riding School

The riding lessons offered at Vintage Oasis Farm are individual or group English lessons for the true beginner or for those getting back into saddle. Our riding lesson program focuses on a neutral posture that prevents blocking the horses’ movements; enabling a softer contact with the horse. We also offer advanced riding lessons for those wishing to broaden their knowledge and riding skills including lessons for those wishing to test their skills at horse shows in any English discipline.

Novice riders will learn basic riding skills, such as how to tack up, walk, trot, and canter. They will also learn about stable management, how to handle horses on the ground, barn safety, basic equine anatomy and health, grooming, including equipment use and care.

We also offer our students the opportunity to take a half-lease with one of our school horses. The half-lease is a six month commitment which includes a lesson and two rides per week.
