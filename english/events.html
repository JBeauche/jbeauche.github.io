1st and 2nd of July - Introduction to Zootherapy

with Centre de Service Équilibre

Introduction to zootherapy?

Interested in learning more about pet therapy or studying in this field?

Then our “Initiation to Zootherapy” training course is made for you!

The workshop takes place over 2 full days of development that will allow you to develop these skills and live these two days as zootherapists for a day.

This training gives you a certificate of success and the prerequisite to enroll in our training program.

These two days take place here, at Vintage Oasis Farm from 9 am to 4 pm.

The regular rate is $299 plus taxes; a $50 discount is applicable upon registration.

N.B. The content of this workshop will be given in French, however, we will be more than happy to help with any of your translation issues.

To find out more or to register, contact the Centre de Service Équilibre
