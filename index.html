Vintage Oasis Farm is a private family oriented Boarding, Breeding & Training facility which focuses on foundation training across the English disciplines.

Our riding lesson program focuses on a neutral posture that prevents blocking the horses' movements, enabling a softer contact with the horse. We offer a range of services that includes:

  • Lease and half-lease (hunter, jumper, dressage);
  • English riding lessons (beginner, intermediate/advanced, up to level 3 dressage, and hunter/jumper at the silver level);
  • Horse starting, training, and rehabilitation board;
  • Sale of horses performing in various English disciplines and levels;
  • Indoor and outdoor board;
  • Possibility to compete in horse shows.

Our mission is to provide you piece of mind by providing optimal care to every horse with their health and well-being as our top priority.

Vintage Oasis Farm is located in Saint-Jean-Sur-Richelieu, on the south shore of Montreal. We are conveniently located close to highway 30, 10, and 35. Public transport is also available.

Please contact for lease opportunities or any other inquiries.
