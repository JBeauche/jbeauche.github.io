Lessons

Description

Individual English riding lessons for the true beginner or for those getting back into saddle. We also offer advanced riding lessons for those wishing to broaden their knowledge and riding skills including lessons for those wishing to test their skills at horse shows in any English discipline.

Novice riders will learn basic riding skills, such as how to tack up, walk, trot and canter. They will also learn about stable management, how to handle horses from the ground, barn safety, basic equine anatomy and health, grooming, equipment use and care.

Advanced lessons include:

  • Dressage (up to level three);
  • Hunter/Jumper;
  • Eventing.

Pricing:

  • Introduction $70.00 + taxes;
  • Individual $60.00 + taxes;
  • Semi-Private $50.00 + taxes.
